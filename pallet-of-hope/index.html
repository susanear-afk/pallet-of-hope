Our Story

Born from Experience,
Driven by Heart

The Giving Pallet was founded by two clothing liquidators and wholesalers who know the industry inside and out — and one of our founders is an autism mom raising three beautiful children.

We saw firsthand how impossible it is to hold a traditional 9-to-5 when your child needs you for therapy appointments, school pickups, sensory emergencies, and daily caregiving that doesn't follow anyone's schedule.

So we built a solution: donate clothing inventory directly to families and teach them how to resell it — creating flexible, work-from-home income that bends to their life, not the other way around.

Our Mission
"Our mission is to empower low-income single parents and autism families — especially those who face barriers to traditional employment due to the daily demands of caregiving — by donating clothing inventory and providing the business education needed to launch and grow reselling businesses. Because every family deserves a pathway to financial independence and a community that has their back, one pallet at a time."
Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

✅ You May Qualify If You Are:

  • A single parent with low income
  • A parent of a child with autism or special needs
  • Unable to maintain traditional employment due to caregiving
  • Motivated to start or grow a reselling business
  • Willing to share your story and goals

📄 Accepted Verification Documents:

  • IEP (Individualized Education Program)
  • Letter from pediatrician, neurologist, or therapist
  • SSI/SSDI award letter for your child
  • Regional Center enrollment letter
  • EBT/CalFresh card or approval letter
  • Medi-Cal/Medicaid card
  • Most recent tax return (page 1 only)
  • Signed self-declaration letter

There are no wrong answers. We just want to know your heart and your story.
